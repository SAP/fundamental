Base Unit of 4

All spacing is divisible by 4, the list below includes the most common spacing increments we use.

12px | small

20px | regular

40px | medium

100px | large

Understanding Sections & Panels

For common content areas such as lists, tables, cards, or forms, there is a system of sections & panels that work together to create a consistent visual layout.

Sections

Sections are the outter most containers for all content. They can contain any combination of components. Each Section has 40px above the content area and 20px below. All components and information contained within the content area will use their own padding and spacing.



Panels

Panels are always contained inside a section and most commonly used when there are 2 panels side by side inside a section. Card grids, however, follow their own spacing system independent of sections or panels.
